# Vision 2030 (رؤية المملكة 2030)

## Short definition

**Saudi Vision 2030** is the Kingdom's long-term strategic roadmap to diversify the economy, develop public services, and empower society—reducing dependence on oil through programs across government, business, and culture.

## Purpose

Vision 2030 organizes national transformation into strategic pillars and realization programs (e.g., Quality of Life, National Transformation, Human Capability Development) and giga-projects that reshape sectors such as tourism, technology, logistics, and entertainment.

## Key services and concepts

- **Economic diversification** — expanding non-oil GDP and private-sector contribution
- **Vision Realization Programs (VRPs)** — major program clusters with measurable KPIs
- **Giga-projects** — NEOM, Red Sea, Qiddiya, and other flagship initiatives (each with separate governance)
- **Labor and human capital** — Saudization, skills development, and labor-market reform
- **Digital government** — unified services, data-driven policy, and platform consolidation
