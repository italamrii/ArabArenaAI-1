# MISA (وزارة الاستثمار)

## Short definition

**MISA** (Ministry of Investment) is the Saudi government ministry responsible for attracting and regulating investment, licensing investor activities, and improving the investment environment in the Kingdom.

## Purpose

MISA facilitates domestic and foreign investment by issuing investment licenses, supporting investors, coordinating with other ministries, and promoting Saudi Arabia as an investment destination under Vision 2030.

## Key services and concepts

- **Investment licensing** — registration and approval pathways for investors
- **Investor services** — guidance, facilitation, and post-licensing support
- **Sector investment promotion** — targeted initiatives for priority industries
- **Regulatory coordination** — alignment with commercial registration, sector regulators, and national programs
- **Vision 2030 linkage** — foreign direct investment and economic diversification goals
