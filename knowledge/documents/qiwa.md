# Qiwa (قوى)

## Short definition

**Qiwa** is Saudi Arabia's unified digital platform for labor-market services, operated under the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development (HRSD). It connects employers, employees, and government labor processes in one ecosystem.

## Purpose

Qiwa exists to digitize and streamline workforce administration: employment contracts, work permits, visa-related labor processes, establishment compliance, and integration with Saudization (Nitaqat) requirements.

## Key services and concepts

- **Employment contracts** — digital contract creation, authentication, and lifecycle management
- **Work permits and labor mobility** — services tied to legal employment status of workers
- **Establishment management** — employer profiles, branch data, and compliance visibility
- **Nitaqat integration** — Saudization classification and related compliance signals
- **Qiwa / HRSD alignment** — part of the broader national labor-market reform agenda
