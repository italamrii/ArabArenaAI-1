# Monsha'at (منشآت)

## Short definition

**Monsha'at** (General Authority for Small and Medium Enterprises) is the Saudi government body responsible for enabling SMEs, entrepreneurship, and the growth of small and medium enterprises across the Kingdom.

## Purpose

Monsha'at supports founders and SMEs through programs, funding pathways, advisory services, training, and ecosystem partnerships—helping reduce barriers to starting and scaling businesses in Saudi Arabia.

## Key services and concepts

- **SME enablement programs** — workshops, accelerators, and advisory support
- **Entrepreneurship ecosystem** — linkage with incubators, investors, and regional hubs
- **Business development services** — guidance on registration, growth, and compliance awareness
- **Policy and research** — SME statistics, sector studies, and national entrepreneurship initiatives
- **Vision 2030 alignment** — private-sector growth and economic diversification
