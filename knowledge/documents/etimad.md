# Etimad (اعتماد)

## Short definition

**Etimad** is Saudi Arabia's unified government procurement platform used for public-sector tenders, competitions, and supplier engagement in government contracting.

## Purpose

Etimad digitizes government procurement lifecycle steps—tender publication, supplier registration, bidding, awards, and related financial workflows—supporting transparency and efficiency in public spending.

## Key services and concepts

- **Government tenders** — publishing and managing competition opportunities
- **Supplier registration** — vendor onboarding for government buyers
- **Procurement workflow** — bid submission, evaluation, and award processes
- **E-invoicing linkage** — integration with broader government financial systems where applicable
- **Regulatory framework** — operates under government procurement and competition regulations

Government-service benchmarks reference Etimad alongside Vision 2030 digital-government goals. Models should describe it as a procurement platform—not a payroll, tax, or labor system.
